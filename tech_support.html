Google Data Analytics
Capstone Project

A full end-to-end data analysis project completed as the capstone of the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate. Applying the complete analytics workflow from defining the business question through cleaning, querying, visualizing, and presenting real-world findings.

Objectives

This capstone is the final requirement of the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate through Coursera. It demonstrates the ability to apply the full six-phase analytics process — Ask, Prepare, Process, Analyze, Share, and Act — using a real-world dataset to generate actionable insights.

The goal is to present a case study that mirrors what a junior data analyst would deliver in a professional environment: complete documentation, clean reproducible data, structured SQL queries, and a clear narrative built around the findings.

Workflow

01

Ask — Define the Business Question

Identify a real-world scenario and frame clear, measurable questions that the data can answer. Align with stakeholder goals.

02

Prepare — Source & Assess the Data

Locate a relevant public dataset, evaluate its credibility, structure, and any limitations that may affect the analysis.

03

Process — Clean the Data

Remove duplicates, handle nulls, standardize formats, and document every transformation using spreadsheets and SQL.

04

Analyze — Query in BigQuery

Write structured SQL queries to surface patterns, aggregations, and trends. Validate results for accuracy.

05

Share — Visualize in Tableau

Build an interactive Tableau dashboard that communicates findings clearly and accessibly to a non-technical audience.

06

Act — Present Recommendations

Deliver data-backed recommendations in a written case study. Summarize insights, limitations, and next steps.
