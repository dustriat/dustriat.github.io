CS & Engineering
Curriculum Design

A complete scope and sequence built from the ground up for middle school students at Infinity Middle School. Courses span Computer Science, Engineering, Video Production, and Journalism — all aligned to Colorado state standards, district curriculum frameworks, and industry expectations.

Project Overview

Since 2021, I've been designing and continuously refining curriculum for four distinct technology courses at Infinity Middle School, part of the Cherry Creek School District in Aurora, CO. Each course was built to balance technical rigor with accessibility — meeting students where they are while preparing them for high school, college, and careers in technology-driven fields.

All courses are project-based, with students demonstrating mastery through real deliverables: working code, functional prototypes, published video content, and original journalism pieces.

Course Areas

01

Computer Science

Computational thinking, block-based and text-based coding, algorithm design, debugging, and introductory programming concepts aligned to CSTA standards.

02

Engineering & Design

The engineering design process applied through hands-on challenges — prototyping, testing, iteration, and presenting solutions to real-world problems.

03

Video Production

Pre-production planning, camera operation, lighting, audio, post-production editing, and responsible digital publishing for student-created video content.

04

Journalism

News writing, editorial standards, source verification, media literacy, digital publishing, and ethical communication for student journalists.
